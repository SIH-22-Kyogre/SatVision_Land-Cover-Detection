Four deep learning models are proposed for classifying land cover. Three models were trained on RGB bands. The other was trained on all bands of the EuroSAT dataset. 
### RGB Bands

            1. ResNet50 
            2. ResNet152v2
            3. VGG16

### All Bands

            1. Custom CNN
